Neural evolutions. It is a interesting way to apply neural networks.

Instead of relying on traditional backpropagation or massive training datasets, vehicles learn to navigate a track entirely through **Genetic Algorithms** and **Neuroevolution**. 

## Overview
The simulation drops a population of autonomous agents onto a circuit with completely randomized neural weights. Utilizing simulated LIDAR (raycasting) for telemetry, the agents evaluate the distance to track boundaries. Vehicles that crash are eliminated, while the top performers are selected to breed the next generation. Through successive iterations of crossover and mutation, the population evolves optimal racing lines and steering mechanics from scratch.
